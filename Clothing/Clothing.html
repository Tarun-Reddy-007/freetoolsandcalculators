Clothing Tools FAQ

How does the clothes size converter work?

It maps your entered size to equivalent standards across regions (US, UK, EU, Asia) using curated conversion tables.

Are shoe size conversions exact?

They are approximate because brand lasts vary; use results as a guidance baseline and check brand charts when possible.

How does the dress color matcher pick palettes?

It compares your selected color to harmony rules (complementary, analogous, triadic) to suggest visually balanced accent colors.

Do these tools store my data?

No. All calculations and color matching run locally in your browser; nothing is sent or saved.

Can size charts differ by brand?

Yes. Manufacturers use unique cuts; treat conversions as an initial estimate and verify with brand-specific measurements.
