Clothes Size Converter FAQ

How does this clothes size converter work?

Select category, clothing type, origin region and your size. The tool looks up a mapped table and returns equivalent sizes in other major regions (US, UK, EU, JP).

Are international sizes fully standardized?

No. Standards vary by region and garment type. Conversions give typical equivalents but brands can shift measurements.

Why might my converted size still fit differently?

Brand design, fabric stretch, intended fit (slim / relaxed) and vanity sizing cause variation beyond numeric conversion.

How can I improve conversion accuracy?

Measure chest, waist, hips with a tape and compare to brand charts. Use conversions as a starting guide, then refine.

Do kids sizes differ more than adult sizes?

Yes. Growth staging and overlapping age ranges create bigger discrepancies—check measurements for best results.

Should I rely only on converted sizes for online shopping?

Use conversions plus brand charts, reviews mentioning fit, and your measurements to minimize return risk.
