Shoe Size Converter FAQ

How does the shoe size converter determine equivalents?

It uses curated comparison tables aligning common US, UK, EU and JP sizing increments for men, women and kids.

Are shoe sizes fully standardized across brands?

No. Last shape, internal volume and brand-specific grading can vary, so always verify with brand charts.

What is the difference between US and UK shoe sizes?

UK sizes typically run about half a size smaller than US for adults (e.g. US 10 ≈ UK 9.5) though kid conversions use separate scales.

How do I measure my foot for accurate sizing?

Trace your foot on paper at day’s end, measure heel-to longest toe in cm, compare to brand length charts; consider width if available.

Why can EU sizing sometimes feel inconsistent?

EU numbers correlate to foot length in cm but rounding, manufacturing tolerances and style shape introduce variation.

Tips for buying shoes online using converted sizes?

Check return policy, read fit reviews, prefer brands publishing foot length charts, and if between sizes, size up for athletic styles.
