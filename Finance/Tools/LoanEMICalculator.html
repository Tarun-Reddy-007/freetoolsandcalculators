Loan EMI Calculator

About EMI

EMI (Equated Monthly Installment) is the fixed payment you make every month until the loan is repaid. It consists of principal + interest components that shift over time.

  • Formula: EMI = P * r * (1+r)^n / ((1+r)^n - 1)
  • P = Principal, r = monthly interest rate, n = total months
  • Processing fee added to upfront cost; not part of principal (optionally)
  • Extra monthly payments reduce interest & shorten tenure

Loan EMI Calculator FAQ

How is EMI calculated?

EMI uses P * r * (1+r)^n / ((1+r)^n - 1) where P principal, r monthly interest (annual rate / 12 / 100) and n number of months.

What affects EMI amount?

Principal, interest rate, tenure length, processing fees (overall cost) and any extra monthly payments (reduce tenure & interest) impact EMI dynamics.

How do extra payments help?

Extra payments accelerate principal reduction, shortening loan duration and lowering total interest. The calculator estimates months and interest saved.

What is a processing fee?

A processing (origination) fee is a lender charge, usually a small percent of principal, increasing total cost; it is not part of principal for EMI formula.

Can EMI change over time?

For fixed-rate loans EMI stays constant. For variable or floating rates EMI or tenure may adjust when the rate resets.
