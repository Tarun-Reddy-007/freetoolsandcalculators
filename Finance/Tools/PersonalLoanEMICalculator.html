How Personal Loan EMI Works

Personal loan EMIs are usually at higher interest rates and shorter tenures than secured loans. This tool shows monthly EMI, total interest, and lets you explore prepayment impact.

  • Formula: EMI = P * r * (1+r)^n / ((1+r)^n - 1)
  • Optional flat processing fee percent is added as upfront cost
  • Extra monthly prepayment reduces interest & tenure (approximate)
