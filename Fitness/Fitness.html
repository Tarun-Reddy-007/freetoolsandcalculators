Fitness Tools FAQ

How is recommended protein intake calculated?

It applies grams per kilogram ranges adjusted for goal (maintenance, muscle gain, fat loss) and activity level.

Are general daily protein guidelines included?

Yes, ranges (e.g. 0.8–2.2 g/kg) help contextualize targets for lifestyle and training intensity.

How does steps-to-calorie conversion work?

It estimates stride length and applies energy per distance factoring weight to approximate calories.

What influences calories burned per step?

Body weight, pace, terrain and step length all shift energy expenditure estimates.

Is my fitness input data stored?

No—values remain only in local browser memory and are never transmitted.
