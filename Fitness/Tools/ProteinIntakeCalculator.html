Protein Intake Calculator

Why Protein?

Protein supports muscle repair, hormonal signaling, immune function, satiety and metabolic health. Needs scale with lean mass, training stress, energy deficit and age.

General Evidence-Based Ranges

Minimal: 0.8 g/kg (RDA – prevents deficiency).
General Health: 1.0–1.4 g/kg.
Muscle Gain / Active: 1.4–1.8 g/kg.
Fat Loss (lean preservation): 1.6–2.2 g/kg.
High Demand / Athletes: up to ~2.4 g/kg (sometimes transiently).

Individualization matters: consult a professional for medical or clinical conditions.
