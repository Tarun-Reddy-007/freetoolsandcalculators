Steps to Calories Converter

How It Estimates Energy

Calories from steps are approximated using distance (steps × stride) and an activity energy cost (MET-based) scaled by weight. Stride length is estimated if not provided.

Assumptions

Average stride ~0.415 × height (female) or 0.413 × height (male) in cm (converted). MET multipliers vary by pace selection.

Rough estimate. Terrain, biomechanics & efficiency cause variation.
