BMI Calculator

What is BMI?

BMI (Body Mass Index) estimates body fat using height & weight. It helps classify: underweight, normal, overweight or obese.

  • Under < 18.5
  • Normal 18.5-24.9
  • Over 25-29.9
  • Obese ≥ 30

BMI is a screening metric; consult a professional for full assessment.

BMI Calculator FAQ

How do I calculate BMI?

Enter height and weight, choose units, then press Calculate. BMI = weight (kg) / [height (m)]². The tool converts ft/in and lb automatically.

Is BMI accurate for everyone?

BMI is a screening tool, not a diagnostic measure. Muscular individuals, children, seniors, and pregnant people may need professional assessment beyond BMI.

What BMI range is considered healthy?

A normal BMI range for most adults is 18.5 to 24.9. Below 18.5 is underweight, 25–29.9 overweight, and 30+ obese.

How often should I check my BMI?

You can recalc anytime your weight changes significantly or at major health checkpoints (e.g., annual physical). Track trends rather than single values.

How can I improve an unhealthy BMI?

Adopt balanced nutrition, consistent physical activity, quality sleep, hydration, and consult a healthcare professional for personalized guidance.
