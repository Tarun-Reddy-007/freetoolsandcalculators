What is BMR?

BMR (Basal Metabolic Rate) is the number of calories your body needs at rest to maintain vital functions (breathing, circulation, cell repair).

Based on the Mifflin-St Jeor Equation:

  • Male: 10xW + 6.25xH - 5xAge + 5
  • Female: 10xW + 6.25xH - 5xAge - 161

Estimates only. Consult a professional for medical or nutritional guidance.
