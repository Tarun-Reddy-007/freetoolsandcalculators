Ideal Weight Calculator

What Is 'Ideal Weight'?

There is no single perfect number. This tool gives estimates from common formulas plus a BMI-based healthy range to inform—not prescribe—goals.

Formulas Included

Devine (1974), Robinson (1983), Miller (1983), Hamwi (1964) — all linear height models originally for medication dosing or population norms.

Estimates only; body composition, frame, age, ethnicity, muscle mass & health status matter more.

About Ideal Weight Calculator

Ideal weight varies by body frame, bone structure, and composition. Our calculator applies four evidence-based formulas (Devine, Robinson, Miller, Hamwi) to provide ranges accounting for individual variation, offering realistic targets versus one-size-fits-all approaches.

Use ranges as guidance, not absolutes. Goals should consider health markers—blood pressure, cholesterol, fitness—beyond weight alone. Frame size, muscle mass, and body composition matter more than a single target.
