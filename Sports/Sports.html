Sports Tools FAQ

How is running pace calculated?

Pace is time per distance (e.g., minutes per kilometer or mile). The calculator divides total time by distance to produce a uniform pace.

What is the difference between pace and speed?

Pace expresses duration per unit distance (min/km); speed expresses distance per unit time (km/h). They are mathematical inverses.

How can I generate race splits?

Enter your goal distance and target time or pace; consistent per‑unit splits are derived automatically for planning.

What factors affect running pace?

Terrain, elevation, temperature, hydration, fueling, and fitness level all influence sustainable pace.

How do I set a realistic race goal?

Use recent training or time trial benchmarks; adjust for course profile and conditions; include conservative pacing early.
