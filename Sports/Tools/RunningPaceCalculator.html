Running Pace Calculator FAQ

How do I calculate running pace?

Divide total time (in seconds) by distance to get seconds per km or mile, then format as minutes:seconds. The tool automates this and also shows speed.

What is the difference between pace and speed?

Pace expresses time per unit distance (e.g. 5:00 per km); speed expresses distance per unit time (e.g. 12 km/h). They are inverses.

What is a good 5K pace for beginners?

Many beginners target 6:00–7:30 per km (9:40–12:00 per mile). Improvement comes with consistent aerobic training and gradual volume increases.

How can I improve my running pace?

Build an aerobic base, add weekly tempo or interval sessions, strength train to reduce injury risk, prioritize sleep and progressive overload.

Should I train by pace or heart rate?

Both can be useful. Pace helps on flat predictable routes; heart rate adjusts for heat, hills or fatigue. Combining them refines effort control.
