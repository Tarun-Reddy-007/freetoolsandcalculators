Age Calculator FAQ

How does the calculator compute exact age?

It subtracts your Date of Birth from the selected reference date, borrowing months/days as needed to give years, months, and days precisely.

Does it account for leap years?

Yes. Day counts include leap days automatically via calendar math; leap years encountered are tallied.

What is the difference between chronological and biological age?

Chronological age is time elapsed since birth. Biological (or physiological) age reflects body condition and can differ based on lifestyle and health.

How is the life expectancy progress bar calculated?

Your lived years divided by target expectancy (years) × 100%. It provides a simple proportional visualization—not a prediction.

How accurate are totals like minutes or seconds lived?

They are derived from whole days elapsed; leap seconds and time-zone transitions are ignored, so values are approximate but close for practical use.
