Date Difference Calculator FAQ

Is the day count inclusive?

Yes. The calculator adds one day so both the start and end dates are counted (inclusive interval).

How are leap years handled?

Leap days naturally appear in the total because date math uses actual calendar days, so Feb 29 is included when in range.

What are business days?

Business days count Monday–Friday excluding any holidays you enter; weekends optionally excluded depending on your setting.

Can I track a milestone date?

Yes. Enter a percentage to see the calendar date at that point between start and end.

Why is my business day count different?

Differences arise if weekends are included or if holidays overlap weekends. Toggle weekend inclusion and verify holiday formats (YYYY-MM-DD).
