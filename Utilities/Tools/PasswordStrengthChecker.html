Password Strength Checker

How Password Strength Is Evaluated

This tool checks your password for length, use of uppercase/lowercase, numbers, symbols, and common patterns. It gives you a strength score and tips to improve your password security.

  • Checks for minimum length
  • Detects use of numbers, symbols, and mixed case
  • Warns about common patterns
  • Gives actionable tips

No passwords are stored or sent anywhere. All checks are done in your browser.
