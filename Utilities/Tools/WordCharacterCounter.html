Word & Character Counter FAQ

How are words counted?

Words are detected using word boundary matching (alphanumeric sequences) after trimming leading and trailing whitespace.

Do you send my text to a server?

No. All counting logic runs locally in your browser; nothing is stored or transmitted.

Why are my counts different from another tool?

Different tools define words or sentences differently (e.g., handling of hyphenated terms or ellipses), leading to slight variations.

What text lengths are supported?

Practically any length supported by your browser—thousands of words—performance remains fast for typical documents.

How can I use this for SEO?

Check keyword density, keep meta descriptions concise, and monitor readability by tracking sentence and word counts.
